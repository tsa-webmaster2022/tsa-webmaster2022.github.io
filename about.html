In Person Invention Convention

Innovation Station offers an in person event that allows inventors to get their products out there. The platform for your product to reach the right people is at Innovation Station's Launchpad Convention. It is here that you're product will finally get the blast off it deserves!

An Online Space for Innovators

The world changed with COVID, but just like our followers, we invite change. We created an online enviorment that help connect innovators from around the world; we call it The Portal. The portal is a virtual adaptation of our Launchpad Convention, so hop on in and continue to push your ideas!

A Community of Creators

Innovation Station and the events affiliated with it were all created in hopes to create an inviting network of innovators and thinkers that are tryign to reach for the stars. There are too many great ideas out there that don't have the outlet, but Innovation Station's Inventor Connvention and The Portal have given so many innovators the network of partners they needed.
